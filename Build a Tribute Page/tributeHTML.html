Jesus

King of kings and Lord of lords

John 3:16 - For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life.

Here's a time line of Lord Jesus:

  • Birth of Jesus - Born in Bethlehem to Mary and Joseph.
  • Presentation at the Temple - Jesus is presented at the Temple in Jerusalem as an infant.
  • Flight to Egypt - The family flees to Egypt to escape King Herod’s decree to kill newborns.
  • Return to Nazareth - After Herod’s death, the family returns and settles in Nazareth.
  • Childhood in Nazareth - Jesus grows up in Nazareth and is described as wise and favored by God.
  • Temple visit at age 12 - Jesus stays behind in the Temple discussing with teachers during a Passover visit.
  • Baptism by John the Baptist - Jesus is baptized in the Jordan River, marking the beginning of his public ministry.
  • Temptation in the Wilderness - Jesus fasts for 40 days and is tempted by Satan.
  • Public Ministry - Jesus teaches, performs miracles, and gathers disciples throughout Galilee, Judea, and other regions.
  • Sermon on the Mount - Delivers a significant teaching on ethics and the Kingdom of God.
  • Transfiguration - Jesus is transfigured on a mountain, revealing his divine glory to Peter, James, and John.
  • Triumphal Entry into Jerusalem - Jesus enters Jerusalem, greeted by crowds laying palm branches.
  • Last Supper - Celebrates the Passover meal with his disciples, instituting the Eucharist.
  • Arrest and Trial - Jesus is arrested in Gethsemane, tried by Jewish and Roman authorities.
  • Crucifixion - Jesus is crucified at Golgotha, dies, and is buried in a tomb.
  • Resurrection - On the third day, Jesus rises from the dead, appearing to his disciples and others.
  • Ascension - After 40 days, Jesus ascends into heaven.
